About Me

Who Am I?

Hi I'm Kyra Kendall Hi, I'm Kyra Kendall, hailing from the vibrant city of Atlanta, Georgia. I'm a self-taught coder with a passion for turning ideas into digital realities. My journey into the world of technology led me to complete the rigorous 100 Devs program for software engineering, where I honed my coding skills and embraced the challenges of building innovative solutions. In addition to my tech background, I bring over 20 years of experience as a pharmacy technician in retail, call centers, hospitals, and health insurance settings. Throughout my career, I've developed strong technical and customer support skills, excelling in solving complex problems and managing customer inquiries with precision and care. My ability to troubleshoot, adapt to various systems, and ensure smooth operations has been key to delivering high-quality service in high-pressure environments. I'm also the proud owner of K. Rai'Ella Designs, my graphic design business. Here, I leverage my creativity to craft visually stunning and effective designs that make a statement. Whether it's web design, branding, or print materials, I'm dedicated to bringing ideas to life with a touch of artistic flair. As I continue to grow and evolve in both the healthcare and tech industries, I'm excited to share my projects and insights with you here on my portfolio. I look forward to combining my technical skills, healthcare expertise, and passion for coding in new and innovative ways.
